Volt Energy Drink

This project was developed for both a Motion Design and an Design & Image class at Fanshawe College.

Objective

The main objective of this project was to create the visual identity of an energy drink. The studentd got a list of fictional energy drink names and prices, and had to choose one and develop the indentity focusing on the price and it's target audience.

Result

I chose the Volt energy drink, that was at the high end of the price range. I decided to focus on a more premium target audience, so I used less colors and darker tones to diferentiate the product from the more popular brands. I used few elements, focusing on the typography and a pattern on the sides.
