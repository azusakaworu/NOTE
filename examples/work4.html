The Best Pizza Price

This group project was developed for a Visual Communication class at Fanshawe College.

Objective

The goal of this assignment was to create the visual identity and develop the design for at least two screens for a pizza related mobile app. The objective was to research and come up with an unique app that didn't exist on the market yet. It was a first study of UI and UX.

Result

The Best Pizza Price would be an app to help people find which restaurant has the best price. We decided on vivid colors to draw atention, and focused on imagery of mouth watering pizzas to appeal to the user. The unique feature of this app would be being building your own pizza and being able to find a place that sells exatcly what you are looking for with the best possible deal.
