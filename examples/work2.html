Skippy Peanut Butter Stout

The project was developed for an Image and Design class at Fanshawe College.

Objective

The objective was to develop a beer version of a famous brand, Skippy, the peanut butter brand, in my case. We could only use bottles, and not cans, and had to create the label and present it as best as possible.

Result

For the logo, I decided to keep the brand's main logo and add a banner, trying to follow their identity as best as possible. I decided on colors that didn't stray far from their personality, but tried to give it a more adult feel.
